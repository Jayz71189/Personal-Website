About Me

Systems and Software Engineer

Hi, my name is Jason Zheng and I am an Engineer with over 10 years of experience, specializing in the aerospace/defense industry. I enjoy the physical mechanics of how things work and I have always have a passion for technology for as long as I can remember. This desire to learn propelled me to learn software coding in addition to my STEM skills in order to utilize new technology and implement them to create new functionality within a system.
