Hi, my name is Jason, I am a Systems and Software Engineer.

I am a Systems Engineer with over 10 years of interdisciplinary engineering experience, specializing in the aerospace/defense industry. In addition to my professional experience, I recently completed a Software Engineering Coding Bootcamp at App Academy, where I honed my technical skills and expanded my knowledge in areas like JavaScript, Python, SQL, HTML/CSS, and Software Testing.

I enjoy the physical mechanics of how things work and I have always have a passion for technology for as long as I can remember. This desire to learn propelled me to learn software coding in addition to my STEM skills in order to utilize new technology and implement them to create new functionality within a system.

During my spare time, I enjoy working on building new applications/websites, but also participating in healthy exercises such as hiking and playing sports especially basketball. I am motivated about teaching the next generation how to stay healthy and live a healthy lifestyle.
